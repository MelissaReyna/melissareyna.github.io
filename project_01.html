About the project

Victoriaville is a captivating town located in the heart of Quebec, Canada. This charming destination harmoniously merges its natural beauty with a rich cultural heritage and a commitment to sustainability. With its tranquil landscapes and vibrant community, Victoriaville offers a unique and inviting experience for both residents and visitors. From its historical roots to its modern aspirations, this town strikes a perfect balance between tradition and innovation, making it a truly captivating place to explore.

In this project, I show you how the transformation of Victoriaville takes shape, while respecting its essence of nature, sustainability, and culture that defines it, but blending it with an innovative touch. It has been an exciting and enriching challenge, as creativity flows freely, making the process follow an unpredictable path. This project aims to boost tourism in Victoriaville, by creating an engaging and visually captivating campaign and mobile app.
